* HOW CAN I SAVE AN ATTRACTION?

You can favorite an attraction by pressing the heart in the upper right corner of the attraction photo. Navigate to the favorites-page, that shows the attractions you've favorited, using the navigations bar at the bottom


* CAN I MARK THE PLACES I'VE ALREADY BEEN TO

Yes. Just press the check-box in the upper left corner of the attraction photo. Navigate to the visited-page, that shows all the attractions you've already seen, using the navigations bar at the bottom
